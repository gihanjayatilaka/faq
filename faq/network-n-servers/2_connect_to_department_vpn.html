1. Visit https://vpn.ce.pdn.ac.lk/ and log into it using your department server account ( eYYXXX )
2. Once you login successfully, download the VPN Client app according to your operating system.
3. Open the installed VPN client and enter vpn.ce.pdn.ac.lk in the Server URL field and click Add Server. Once you do that, you will be redirected to the VPN portal where you can login using your credentials.
4. Click Approve in the VPN portal in order to connect to the VPN. (Login, if it is srequired)
5. Verify Connectivity by Pinging 10.40.18.1
6. Congratulations! You are now connected to CE VPN.
